📞 Help & Support

National Cybercrime Helpline

📞 Call 1930 (Toll-free and available 24x7)

This helpline helps in blocking suspicious transactions if reported quickly after a fraud.

Report Cybercrime Online

🌐 Visit the official portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Steps to File a Complaint

  1. Collect evidence (screenshots, emails, messages, transaction ID)
  2. Go to cybercrime.gov.in
  3. Click on “File a Complaint”
  4. Choose either:
    • “Report Other Cyber Crime” (for financial, scams, malware, etc.)
    • “Report Women/Child-related Crime” (for personal threats)
  5. Login using your mobile number and OTP
  6. Fill in the complaint form and upload your evidence
  7. Submit and note down your complaint reference number

Additional Support

  • 📧 RBI Phishing Complaints: report.phishing@rbi.org.in
  • 🛍️ Consumer Grievances (shopping/job frauds): consumerhelpline.gov.in
  • 👮 Visit your local Cyber Police Station if the matter is urgent or serious
