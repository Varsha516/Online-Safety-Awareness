🌐 Online Scams & Offers

Common Online Scams

  • Online Shopping Fraud: Fake websites or sellers take your money and never deliver the product.
  • Job Offer Scams: Fake recruiters ask for registration or training fees but vanish afterward.
  • Fake Loan Apps: Fraudulent apps offer quick loans with hidden charges and data theft.
  • Cryptocurrency Scams: Promise high returns in Bitcoin or crypto but vanish with your investment.
  • Fake Charity Scams: Emotional messages during disasters trick you into donating to fake causes.

How to Stay Safe

  • Buy from trusted platforms only (Amazon, Flipkart, official sites).
  • Verify job or loan offers before paying any amount.
  • Avoid clicking on social media ads without background checks.
  • Be skeptical of “too good to be true” offers.
  • Donate only to verified NGOs or government-listed disaster relief funds.

What To Do If You’re Scammed

  • Take screenshots of ads, messages, receipts, or profiles involved.
  • Immediately block the sender or fraudulent page/account.
  • Contact your bank or wallet provider if any money was paid.

Report Online Scam

📞 Call Cyber Helpline: 1930

🌐 To report online:

  1. Go to https://cybercrime.gov.in
  2. Click on “File a Complaint”
  3. Select “Report Other Cyber Crime”
  4. Login using your mobile number and OTP
  5. Submit your complaint with screenshots or payment details

🧾 You can also file a consumer grievance at:
consumerhelpline.gov.in

Be Cyber Smart

Every click matters. Whether it's an offer, a job, or a charity appeal — always pause and verify. Scammers prey on emotion and urgency. Your best defense is awareness and skepticism.
