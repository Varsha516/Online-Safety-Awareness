📱 Mobile & App-Based Threats

Common Mobile Threats

  • Fake Apps: Lookalike apps that steal data or show fake content.
  • App Clones: Fraudulent versions of apps like Paytm, PhonePe, GPay.
  • Excessive Permissions: Apps that request access to camera, mic, or contacts unnecessarily.
  • Spyware-infected Apps: Secretly record activity, steal SMS or banking info.

How to Stay Safe

  • Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  • Check app reviews, developer name, and permissions before installing.
  • Revoke unnecessary permissions from existing apps.
  • Avoid downloading APKs from unknown websites.
  • Keep your phone's software up to date.

What to Do If You’re Affected

  • Uninstall the suspicious app immediately.
  • Clear cache/data related to the app and reset app permissions.
  • Scan your phone with a trusted mobile security app (e.g., Norton, Bitdefender, Avast).
  • Change passwords of affected accounts.
  • Inform your bank if any payment or personal data was involved.

Report Mobile App Fraud

📞 Call Cybercrime Helpline: 1930

🌐 Report Online:

  1. Go to https://cybercrime.gov.in
  2. Click on "File a Complaint"
  3. Select "Report Other Cyber Crime"
  4. Log in with your mobile number and follow the form instructions

🛑 Also report malicious apps directly to the app store:

  • Google Play: Open the app → Tap three dots → Report as inappropriate
  • Apple App Store: Visit the app page → Scroll to "Report a Problem"

Be Smart on Mobile

Your phone knows everything about you — keep it protected like a digital vault. One wrong app can give hackers full access to your private life. Stay alert, verify every app, and think before you tap.
